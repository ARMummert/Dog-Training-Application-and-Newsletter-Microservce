Petiquette Training 101

Training your dog can be a challenge. Dogs, especially puppies, have their eyes set on anything that catches their attention. This can be avoided entirely with good training and obedience skills.

We value our members and encourage four words to remember when actively training your dog: Patience, Persistence, Love, Food

We offer dog training methods that guide you through training you dog. Each method provides a step-by-step guide for you to train efficiently.



Command and Reward System

We encourage use of our Command and Reward System when you first start training your dog. You can slowly remove the reward as training progresses so your dog doesn't become reward reliant.

Rewards can range from your dog's favoriate treat or a favorite toy you only give to your dog during training sessions. Toys and play time as a reward can be highly motivating for your dog. Using a clicker while training is another good option.
